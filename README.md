# Asteroid Game

## Description
The Asteroid Game is an exciting multiplayer space game developed by NASA, where players navigate their spacecraft through an asteroid field while competing against each other. The objective is to survive as long as possible while collecting points by dodging asteroids and using power-ups.

## How to Play
1. Open the game by visiting [Play Asteroid Game](https://tech-by-nasa.github.io/asteroid/).
2. Use the arrow keys to navigate your spacecraft.
3. Collect resources and avoid colliding with asteroids.
4. Compete with other players to achieve the highest score!

## Features
- Multiplayer gameplay: Challenge your friends or compete against players worldwide.
- Stunning graphics and sound effects.
- Various power-ups to enhance your gaming experience.
- Leaderboards to track your progress and compare scores.

## How to Customize
To customize your gameplay experience, you can:
- Adjust the settings in the game menu.
- Change the appearance of your spacecraft by selecting different skins available in the game.
